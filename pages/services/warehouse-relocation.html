Warehouse Relocation Services

Moving facilities? We handle the complete relocation of your racking systems—teardown, transport, reconfiguration, and reinstallation at your new location.

Warehouse relocation involves moving your racking systems from one facility to another. This includes teardown at the origin, transportation to the destination, and reinstallation in the new space—often with layout modifications to fit the new building. Rack Storage Solutions provides end-to-end relocation services with project management that minimizes downtime and ensures your new facility is ready when you need it.

Relocation Options

Full Service

We handle everything: assessment, teardown, transport, and installation at the new location.

Best for: Complex moves, tight timelines, operations that need to minimize internal resource commitment.

Teardown + Install

We disassemble and reinstall; you coordinate transportation between facilities.

Best for: Short-distance moves where you have trucking resources or want to use your preferred carrier.

Labor Only

You manage the project; we provide professional crews for specific tasks.

Best for: Projects where you have internal project management but need skilled labor.
