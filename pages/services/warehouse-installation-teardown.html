Warehouse installation and teardown services include the professional assembly, anchoring, reconfiguration, and disassembly of pallet racking systems. Rack Storage Solutions provides experienced installation crews who handle everything from small rack additions to complete warehouse buildouts. Our crews are fully insured, trained in rack-specific safety practices, and available for projects nationwide.

What We Do

New Installation

Complete assembly of new or used racking systems. We handle unloading, sorting, assembly, leveling, anchoring, and final inspection. All installations meet manufacturer specifications and local code requirements.

Teardown / Disassembly

Safe removal of existing racking. We disassemble systematically, organize components for reuse or disposal, and leave your space clean. Teardown is essential for relocations or decommissioning facilities.

Reconfiguration

Modifying existing layouts to accommodate new products, processes, or efficiency improvements. We can relocate rows, adjust beam heights, add sections, or completely reorganize your floor plan.

Repairs & Modifications

Replacing damaged uprights or beams, adding safety accessories, installing wire decking, or making other modifications to existing systems. We assess damage and recommend appropriate repairs.

Why Professional Installation Matters

Rack installation isn't just assembly—it's ensuring your system is safe, level, and up to code.

Safety

Improperly installed rack can collapse, causing injury, product damage, and liability. Our crews follow proper procedures every time.

Code Compliance

Anchoring requirements, seismic bracing, and plumb/level tolerances vary by jurisdiction. We know the requirements and install accordingly.

Efficiency

Experienced crews work faster than general labor. A job that might take your team a week could be done in days.

Right Tools

Scissor lifts, powder-actuated anchor tools, laser levels—professional crews have the equipment to do the job right.

What's Included in Installation

Pre-Install Verification

Unloading & Sorting

Assembly

Leveling

Final Walkthrough

Installing in Operating Facilities

Many clients need installation without shutting down operations. We have experience working alongside active warehouse operations.

How We Work Around Your Operation

  • Phased installation: We install in sections, completing and releasing areas for use while working on others.
  • Safety barriers: Physical barriers separate work zones from active areas to protect workers and inventory.
  • Off-hours work: Second shifts, weekends, or overnight work available to minimize disruption.
  • Coordination: Daily communication with your team about work areas and timing.
  • Clean work practices: We keep work areas organized and clean up as we go.
