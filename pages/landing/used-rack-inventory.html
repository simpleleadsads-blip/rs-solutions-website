Understanding Our Grades

Grade A

Like-new condition. Minimal cosmetic wear. Uniform color. May have been lightly used or is overstock.

Grade B

Good condition. Normal cosmetic wear—minor scratches, paint chips. Structurally sound. Colors may vary.

Grade C

Economy grade. Noticeable cosmetic wear, surface rust. Structurally acceptable. Maximum value.

All grades maintain full structural capacity. Cosmetic differences only.
