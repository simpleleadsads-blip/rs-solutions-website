When Do You Need Permits?

Usually Required

  • Rack over 8-12 feet high (varies by location)
  • Any installation in California
  • High seismic zones (SDC D, E, F)
  • Storage of hazardous materials
  • Mezzanines or rack-supported platforms

Usually Not Required

  • Short shelving under 6 feet
  • Low seismic zones with low rack
  • Some jurisdictions under 8 feet

Note: We always verify with local building department
