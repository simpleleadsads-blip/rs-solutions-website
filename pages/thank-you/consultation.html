How to Prepare

To make the most of our consultation, it helps to have the following ready:

  • Warehouse dimensions - Square footage, ceiling height, column spacing
  • Product information - What you're storing, typical pallet weights, pallet sizes
  • Current challenges - What's not working with your current setup (if applicable)
  • Goals - Pallet positions needed, workflow improvements, timeline
  • Floor plan - If you have a CAD drawing or even a sketch, it helps

Don't worry if you don't have everything—we'll work with what you have.
