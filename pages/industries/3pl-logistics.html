Racking Configurations for 3PL Operations

Selective Rack Zones

The backbone of most 3PL operations. Direct access to every pallet position supports diverse SKU profiles and high throughput requirements.

  • 100% selectivity for any pallet, any time
  • Easy beam height adjustment as client needs change
  • Supports FIFO rotation for date-sensitive inventory
  • Compatible with standard forklifts and reach trucks

Best for: High-SKU clients, mixed inventory, pick-and-pack operations

High-Density Zones

For clients with deep, homogeneous inventory, high-density configurations increase pallet positions per square foot.

  • Push-Back: 2-6 deep storage with LIFO access from aisle
  • Drive-In: Maximum density for low-SKU, high-volume
  • Pallet Flow: Automatic FIFO rotation for date-code compliance
  • Double-Deep: 2-deep selective accessed by reach truck

Best for: Bulk storage clients, food & beverage, high-volume/low-SKU

Hybrid Layouts

Most 3PL operations benefit from combining rack types—selective for active pick areas, high-density for bulk reserve.

  • Ground-level selective with push-back in upper levels
  • Dedicated high-density zones for specific clients
  • Carton flow integrated for case-pick operations
  • Modular design allows reconfiguration as needs change

Best for: Multi-client facilities with diverse storage requirements

Narrow Aisle Systems

When every square foot counts, narrow or very narrow aisle configurations maximize pallet positions without sacrificing selectivity.

  • Narrow Aisle: 8-10' aisles with reach trucks
  • Very Narrow Aisle: 5-6' aisles with turret trucks
  • Up to 50% more storage than conventional wide aisle
  • Maintains 100% selectivity

Best for: Expensive real estate markets, capacity expansion without moving
