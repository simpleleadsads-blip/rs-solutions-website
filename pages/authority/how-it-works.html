How It Works

From your first call to a fully operational storage system, here's what to expect when you work with Rack Storage Solutions.

Working with Rack Storage Solutions follows a straightforward process: consultation to understand your needs, design and quoting, permitting if required, equipment procurement, and professional installation. We handle the complexity so you can focus on your operation. Most projects from first contact to completed installation take 3-6 weeks depending on scope and permitting requirements.

Our Process

1

Initial Consultation

It starts with a conversation. You tell us about your project—what you're storing, your warehouse dimensions, how your operation works, and what you're trying to accomplish.

What we discuss:

  • Product types, sizes, and weights
  • Warehouse dimensions and clear height
  • Current pain points or goals
  • Budget parameters
  • Timeline requirements

Timeline: Usually 30-60 minutes by phone or video

2

Design & Quote

Based on our consultation, we create a custom layout for your space and provide detailed pricing. For complex projects, we may offer multiple options to compare.

What you receive:

  • Floor plan layout drawing
  • Pallet position count and capacity specs
  • Itemized equipment pricing
  • Installation pricing
  • Estimated timeline

Timeline: 1-5 business days depending on complexity

3

Permitting (If Required)

If your jurisdiction requires permits for rack installation, we handle the process. We determine requirements, prepare engineering documents, and submit applications.

What we handle:

  • Researching local permit requirements
  • PE-stamped engineered drawings
  • Seismic calculations (where required)
  • Permit application submission
  • Responding to plan check comments

Timeline: 1-4 weeks depending on jurisdiction

Note: Not all projects require permits. We'll let you know if yours does.

4

Procurement & Delivery

Once you approve the quote (and permits are secured if applicable), we procure your equipment and coordinate delivery to your site.

What happens:

  • Equipment ordered from inventory or manufacturers
  • Quality verification before shipment
  • Freight coordination to your location
  • Delivery scheduling with your team

Timeline: 1-3 weeks depending on equipment source

5

Installation

Our professional crews arrive on schedule and install your system. We handle everything from unloading to final walkthrough.

What's included:

  • Material unloading and organization
  • Assembly per manufacturer specifications
  • Leveling and alignment
  • Floor anchoring
  • Final walkthrough with you
  • Work area cleanup

Timeline: 1-5 days for most projects

Typical Project Timeline

Consultation & Quote 3-5 days
Design Finalization 2-3 days
Permitting (if required) 1-4 weeks
Equipment Procurement 1-3 weeks
Installation 1-5 days
Total (without permits): 3-4 weeks
Total (with permits): 5-8 weeks

*Timelines vary based on project complexity, equipment availability, and local permit processing times
